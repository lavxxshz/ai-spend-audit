# Unit Economics

## What a Converted Lead is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI credits. Average deal size estimate:
- Small startup (10 people, $500/mo AI spend) → saves 30% → $150/mo savings → Credex takes ~15% margin → **$270/year per customer**
- Medium startup (50 people, $3000/mo AI spend) → saves 30% → $900/mo savings → Credex takes ~15% margin → **$1,620/year per customer**

Blended average customer LTV: **$800/year** (conservative estimate)
Average contract length: 18 months
Blended LTV: **$1,200 per converted customer**

## Conversion Funnel

- Audit completed → Email captured: **20%**
- Email captured → Credex page clicked: **15%**
- Credex page clicked → Consultation booked: **10%**
- Consultation booked → Credit purchase: **40%**

Overall: Audit completed → Purchase = **0.20 × 0.15 × 0.10 × 0.40 = 0.12%**

At 10,000 audits/month → 12 purchases/month → $14,400 MRR
